# Product vision

Wikipedia races show that navigating knowledge can be playful, but ordinary hyperlinks hide the
meaning of a connection. Webwoven replaces that ambiguity with a curated, typed graph.

## Audience

Webwoven is for curious learners who enjoy puzzles, trivia, and discovering unexpected
connections. The Build Week release is English-only and works on desktop and mobile web without
requiring an account.

## Experience principles

1. Every move is explainable and attributable.
2. Route quality matters more than raw speed.
3. The server, not the client or generated prose, decides truth and scoring.
4. Open data is frozen into reviewed, reproducible graph versions.
5. Typography and information design carry the experience; decorative technology tropes do not.

## Build Week boundary

Included: Solo Route Race, Daily Connection, Live Relay, three deterministic hints, guest
profiles, sources, and leaderboards. Deferred: accounts, freeform round creation, quizzes, hidden
targets, exact-route puzzles, shops, chat, and additional datasets.
